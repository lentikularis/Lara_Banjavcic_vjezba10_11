Pictures of the planets in our Solar System

Our Solar System is a spiral galaxy called Milky Way



Sun


Though Sun is not really a planet, it is the star at the center of the Solar System.

Mercury


Mercury is the first planet from the Sun and the smallest planet in the Solar System.

Venus


Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It is a rocky planet, has an atmosphere, which is by far the thickest of all rocky Solar System bodies

Earth


Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only place known in the universe where life has originated and found habitability.

Mars


Mars is the fourth planet out from the Sun. It is a dusty, cold, desert world with a very thin atmosphere.

Jupiter


Jupiter is the biggest planet in our solar system. It's similar to a star, but it never got big enough to start burning.

Saturn


Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest planet in our solar system.

Uranus


Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun, and has the third-largest diameter in our solar system. It was the first planet found with the aid of a telescope.

Neptune


Neptune is one of two ice giants in the outer solar system.The ice giant Neptune was the first planet located through mathematical calculations.
